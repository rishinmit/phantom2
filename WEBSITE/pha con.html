Phantom Continental

Behind the wheels, drivers embark on a journey reminiscent of a golden age in motoring. Testament to the artistry and sophistication of vintage motoring. With its captivating design, luxurious interior, and timeless performance.




Powertrain and Engine Specifications:

Engine Type: Inline-Six
Displacement: 3,500 cc
Aspiration: Naturally Aspirated
Compression Ratio: 9.5:1
Fuel Delivery: Carbureted (Twin Carburetors)

Performance Metrics:

Horsepower: 180 HP
Torque: 250 Nm
Acceleration (0-60 mph): 8.5 seconds
Top Speed: 120 mph

Transmission and Drivetrain:

Transmission Type: 4-Speed Manual
Gear Ratios: 1st: 3.50
2nd: 2.00
3rd: 1.30
4th: 1.00
Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive (RWD)

Chassis and Suspension Details:

Chassis Type: Body-on-Frame
Suspension System: Double-Wishbone Front, Solid Axle Rear
Shock Absorbers: Bilstein Classic Shock Absorbers
Braking System: Hydraulic Drum Brakes (Front and Rear)

Exterior Features:

Body Material: Steel
Exterior Dimensions: Length: 4,800 mm
Width: 1,800 mm
Height: 1,500 mm
Aerodynamics: Classic Aerodynamic Design with Chrome Accents
