Phantom G7 Comet

Boasts a sleek and streamlined design that slices through the air with minimal resistance. Every curve and contour is meticulously engineered to optimize airflow, enhancing stability and speed during high-velocity races.




Powertrain and Engine Specifications:

Engine Type: V8 Twin-Turbocharged
Displacement: 4,000 cc
Aspiration: Twin-Turbocharged
Compression Ratio: 10.5:1
Fuel Delivery: Direct Fuel Injection

Performance Metrics:

Horsepower: 650 HP
Torque: 750 Nm
Acceleration (0-60 mph): 3.2 seconds
Top Speed: 210 mph

Transmission and Drivetrain:

Transmission Type: 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic
Gear Ratios: 1st: 3.30
2nd: 2.15
3rd: 1.55
4th: 1.20
Drivetrain: All-Wheel Drive (AWD)

Chassis and Suspension Details:

Chassis Type: Carbon Fiber Monocoque
Suspension System: Double-Wishbone Front and Rear
Shock Absorbers: Adjustable Electronic Shock Absorbers
Braking System: Carbon Ceramic Disc Brakes (Front and Rear)

Exterior Features:

Body Material: Carbon Fiber
Exterior Dimensions: Length: 4,500 mm
Width: 2,000 mm
Height: 1,200 mm
Aerodynamics: Advanced Aerodynamic Design with Active Aero Elements
